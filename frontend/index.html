Shelf Visibility Intelligence

Count visible products from a single shelf photo.

ShelfVision is a simple hackathon prototype for shelf monitoring. Upload one shelf image and the system estimates visible product facings, highlights low-stock gaps, and gives a quick shelf health score.

Out-of-stock detection Flags large visible gaps before they become missed sales.
Visible quantity estimate Counts visible product facings instead of trying to identify exact brands.
Hackathon-ready dashboard Shows counts, gaps, alerts, and annotated output in one place.
